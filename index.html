Candidates

A candidate is a representative of a particular political party that becomes a contestant for elections in any of the constituency. His or Her job is to exhibit the views and ideologies of the concerned political party and to ensure the voters are convinced so much so that they vote for that candidate in their constituency and thus make the political party victorious.

Voters

The most integral part of any democracy are its voters. These are the population that casts their votes in order to conduct elections. Voters decide the outcomes of an election by determining which candidate would win from which constituency and ultimately which political party or coalition would form the Government.
